Terms of Service

These terms explain how Co-op Station works and what to expect when using the platform.

Using Co-op Station

Co-op Station is a student-focused platform designed to help university students discover cooperative training opportunities from multiple trusted sources in one place. By using the platform, you agree to use it responsibly and in line with its purpose.

Our Role

We collect, organize, and display co-op opportunities to make them easier to find. Co-op Station does not offer training positions and does not make hiring or acceptance decisions. Our role is limited to providing access to information.

Applications and External Websites

When you choose to apply for an opportunity, you will be redirected to the official website of the organization offering the training. Applications are handled entirely by those organizations. Co-op Station is not responsible for external websites, their content, or application outcomes.

User Contributions

Students may submit opportunities to help other users. When submitting content, you are responsible for ensuring that the information is accurate and up to date.

To keep the platform useful and reliable, submissions may be reviewed, edited, approved, or removed by the admin team if they are outdated, duplicated, or misleading.

Responsible Use

Users are expected to interact with the platform in a respectful and lawful manner. Misuse of the platform, including sharing false information or attempting to disrupt the service, may result in restricted access.

Accuracy of Information

While we aim to keep opportunities accurate and current, details may change over time. We encourage users to always verify information directly on the official company website before applying.

Platform Updates

Co-op Station may update, improve, or modify features of the platform to enhance the user experience. These terms may also be updated when necessary.

Contact Us

If you have questions about these terms or the platform, you can contact the Co-op Station team at coopstation.team@gmail.com .
