About Co-op Station

Co-op Station is built by university students who noticed a real gap in how co-op opportunities are shared—scattered across many platforms and easy to miss.

Centralized ✓ Verified community collaboration

Why We Built It

We created Co-op Station to make discovering co-op opportunities simpler, more organized, and less time-consuming for students.

What We Do

  • Collect co-op opportunities in one place so students can browse and search easily.
  • Allow students to submit opportunities that are reviewed before publishing.
  • Redirect students to official company websites to apply safely.

Keeping It Reliable

All opportunities go through review, duplicate checks, and community reporting to keep the platform trustworthy and up to date.
