Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data when you use Co-op Station.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Co-op Station is built as a student-focused platform. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your information in a transparent and responsible way. We only collect the data necessary to operate and improve the platform.

Information We Collect

When you use Co-op Station, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information, such as your name and email address when you create an account.
  • User activity data, such as opportunities you submit, bookmark, or report.
  • Technical information, such as basic usage data needed to keep the platform running smoothly (for example, page visits or error logs).

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To display and manage co-op opportunities on the platform.
  • To review submissions and maintain content quality.
  • To improve platform performance, usability, and reliability.
  • To communicate with you when necessary regarding platform-related matters.

Sharing of Information

Co-op Station does not sell, rent, or trade user information. Your data is not shared with third parties except when required to operate the platform (such as hosting or technical services) or when required by law.

External Links

Co-op Station contains links to external websites operated by companies offering co-op opportunities. When you leave our platform, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external websites you visit.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and users should be aware of this when sharing information online.

Your Choices and Control

You have control over your personal information. Depending on platform features, you may:

  • Update your account information.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Contact us with questions or concerns about your data.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect platform changes or legal requirements. Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact the Co-op Station team at coopstation.team@gmail.com .
