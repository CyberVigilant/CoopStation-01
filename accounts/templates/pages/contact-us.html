Contact Us

Have a question or need support? You’ll likely find your answer below. If not, feel free to reach out to the Co-op Station team.

Common Questions

How do I apply for an opportunity?
Co-op Station does not handle applications directly. Clicking “Apply” will redirect you to the company’s official website.
Can I submit an opportunity I found?
Yes. Students can submit opportunities through the platform. All submissions are reviewed before publishing.
Why was my submitted opportunity rejected?
Submissions may be rejected if they are expired, duplicated, missing key details, or from unreliable sources.
How can I report an outdated or suspicious opportunity?
Use the “Report” option on the opportunity page. Reports are reviewed by the admin team.
Does Co-op Station scrape LinkedIn or X?
No. We avoid scraping restricted platforms. Opportunities may appear through manual admin submission only.
